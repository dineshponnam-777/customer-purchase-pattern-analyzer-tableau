# 📊 Customer Purchase Pattern Analyzer

## 📌 Project Overview

The Customer Purchase Pattern Analyzer is an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze customer buying behavior, spending patterns, customer segmentation, and product performance.

This project simulates real-world retail and e-commerce analytics used by organizations such as Amazon, Flipkart, BigBasket, and Walmart to improve customer retention, increase revenue, and identify high-value customer groups.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to:

- Analyze customer spending behavior
- Identify high-value customers
- Evaluate product category performance
- Understand regional purchasing trends
- Monitor customer loyalty and repeat purchases
- Generate actionable business insights

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Tableau Public
- Microsoft Excel / CSV Dataset
- Data Visualization
- Customer Analytics
- Retail Business Intelligence

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Customer Segments

- At Risk customers generated the highest revenue contribution.
- Frequent Buyers showed strong repeat purchasing behavior.
- High Value customers contributed significantly to overall revenue.

### Product Categories

- Electronics generated the highest sales.
- Fashion and Home Appliances followed closely.
- Sports category generated the lowest spending.

### Customer Loyalty

- 75.40% repeat customer rate indicates strong customer retention.
- Customer satisfaction score of 4.02 reflects a positive customer experience.

### Regional Analysis

- Spending patterns vary across major cities.
- Ahmedabad and Mumbai show strong customer purchasing activity.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

✅ Launch loyalty programs for Frequent Buyers.

✅ Re-engage At Risk customers through personalized offers.

✅ Increase promotions in lower-performing categories.

✅ Expand successful product categories with targeted marketing campaigns.

✅ Utilize regional insights for location-based marketing strategies.
